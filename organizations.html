Flow It Forward

Who Your Donation Helps

Lifting Hands International is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing humanitarian aid and support to refugees and displaced populations around the world.
Flow It Forward supports Lifting Hands International by donating menstrual hygiene supplies to aid their humanitarian efforts. Last year alone, Flow It Forward delivered over 5,000 pads to Lifting Hands International's warehouse, contributing to their mission of providing essential aid to refugees. Flow It Forward bolsters the impact of Lifting Hands International's work by helping to address a vital aspect of women's health and hygiene in refugee populations.
