How To Donate

Flow It Forward accepts donations via drop off, local pick up, and Venmo. Venmo offers a flexible approach that allows individuals to support the cause financially, ensuring that vital resources and assistance can still be provided to those in need. Whether it's due to logistical constraints or personal preference, this option enables everyone to participate in making a positive impact.

Venmo

To make a donation via Venmo, there are two convenient options available. You can either visit the Venmo website, or click on the qr code provided above. For mobile users, simply tap and hold on the QR code provided above. My Venmo username is @styleoli, and your contributions are greatly appreciated. Please denote pads or menstrual hygiene products in the description box ("What's this for?"). Thank you for your generosity and support!
Those who wish to donate physical items directly can reach out to us on Instagram, @flowitforwardwithus, to arrange pick up or drop off.
